Study Skill Guide

Welcome to the academic success skills website. This site is a resource that will help you develop learning strategies structured to your individual needs. Maya Angelou once wrote:

Do the Best you can until you know better. Then when you know better, do better.
This quote was said in the context of survival and living your best life. It is important for all of us to understand ourselves and why we do the things we do. However, success doesnâ€™t come from you alone. This website will help you find your supportive relationships to help make you a better student and reach your long-term goals. You will discover learning methods that will improve your study habits. Throughout this site, various topics related to individual learning will help you better understand how you learn. Each section will identify resources and support networks that will enhance your educational experience.

Goals and Objectives

The goals for this website are: 1. Learn academic strategies/study skills for test taking, time management, reading and writing skills, and organization 2.Strengthen social support by building strong relationships inside and outside the classroom 3.Understand personal learning styles and strengths using the My True Color page 4.Identify sources of motivation through goal setting 5.Understand the online class format 6. Identify barriers to success; personal and professional
