About the Author

I am accomplished and energetic with a solid history of achievement in higher education. I am a motivated leader with strong organizational skills and the ability to prioritize responsibilities. I am able to manage large groups and am adaptable to change.
I am a loyal employee working long periods for two different universities. I began my working career as part owner of a video production company, creating promotional videos for various commercial entities in the St. Louis area. After several years, I transitioned to higher education beginning as an alumni coordinator, then to assistant registrar, to registrar and director of admissions. I am now the director of student and academic support services, mentoring students in various capacities.

My education includes a Bachelor of Science in Mass Communications from Lindenwood Univeristy, 1983, a Master of Science in Management and Administration from Lindenwood University, 1995 and am currently working on my Ed.S. in education technology from the University of Missouri-Columbia.
